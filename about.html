About Management Mania

Welcome to Management Mania – a web portal designed for the students of the BUP Management Department. This platform makes it easy to find student lists and profiles, helping you stay connected with your batchmates and seniors.

While unofficial, our goal is to bring all Management Department members together in one place. In the future, we plan to include senior students’ information, alumni details, and highlights of those excelling in different fields.

This is just the beginning – let’s see how far we can take it!

Explore, engage, and share your feedback to help us improve. Thanks for being part of the community! ⚜
